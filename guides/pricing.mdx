# Pricing & Plans

PolyHistorical offers three plans. Start free, upgrade when you need more data.

## What "Last N" means

On the Starter plan, market access is limited to the most recent markets of each type:

- **BTC 5m & 15m**: Access the 50 most recent markets
- **BTC 1h & 4h**: Access the 24 most recent markets
- **BTC 24h**: Access the 5 most recent markets

Pro and Enterprise plans have unlimited access to all historical markets.

## Upgrading

1. Log in to your [Dashboard](https://polyhistorical.com/dashboard)
2. Click **Upgrade to Pro**
3. Complete payment -- your plan upgrades instantly

Your new rate limits and market access take effect immediately. No need to regenerate API keys.

## Downgrading

When you cancel a Pro subscription:
- Your plan reverts to Starter at the end of the billing cycle
- Existing API keys continue to work (within Starter limits)
- Historical data access is reduced to Starter limits
